Privacy Policy

Overview

Your privacy is important to us. At YALDY™, we are committed to protecting your personal information and being transparent about how it is collected, used, and safeguarded. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, why we collect it, and how we use it to provide a safe and meaningful experience for families.

Information We May Collect

We may collect the following types of information when you use our website or services:

  • Personal details such as your name, email address, phone number, and shipping address
  • Order and purchase information
  • Payment-related data, processed securely by trusted third-party payment providers
  • Website usage data, including pages visited and interactions, collected through cookies and analytics tools

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children without parental consent.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Process and deliver orders
  • Communicate with you regarding purchases, updates, or customer support
  • Improve our website, products, and user experience
  • Send relevant updates or information you choose to receive
  • Comply with legal and regulatory requirements

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or trade your personal information. Your data may be shared only with trusted third parties such as payment processors, shipping providers, and analytics services, solely for the purpose of operating our business. All third parties are required to keep your information secure and confidential.

Cookies & Analytics

Our website uses cookies and similar technologies to enhance your browsing experience and analyze site traffic. You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings at any time.

Your Rights (EU Residents)

For EU residents: You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). Contact us to exercise these rights.

Your Rights (Israeli Residents)

Under Israeli Privacy Protection Law, you have the right to access and correct your personal data. Contact us to exercise these rights.
