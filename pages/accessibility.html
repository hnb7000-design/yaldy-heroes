Accessibility Statement

This statement was last updated on February 2026.

YALDY™ – Real Heroes. True Strength. – is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for all visitors to our website. We continuously work to improve the accessibility and usability of our site and strive to provide an inclusive, respectful, and meaningful experience for all users, including people with disabilities.

What Web Accessibility Is

Web accessibility allows people with disabilities to browse and interact with websites with the same or similar level of ease and enjoyment as other users. This is achieved through proper website structure, assistive technologies, and inclusive design practices.

Accessibility Adjustments on This Site

We have implemented accessibility adjustments on this website in accordance with WCAG 2.1 guidelines and aim to meet Level AA standards.

As part of our efforts, we have:

  • Used web accessibility best practices and tools to identify and fix accessibility issues
  • Defined clear heading structures across pages
  • Ensured sufficient color contrast
  • Added alternative text to relevant images
  • Enabled keyboard navigation
  • Minimized unnecessary motion and animations
  • Ensured content is accessible where technically possible

Declaration of Partial Compliance

Some features on this website rely on third-party services or platform components (such as payment, checkout, or analytics tools) that are not fully under our direct control. While we make every reasonable effort to ensure accessibility across our site, the accessibility of these components may depend on the standards of the service providers themselves.

About Our Organization

YALDY™ – Real Heroes. True Strength. – operates as an online-based business and does not maintain physical customer service locations. All services and customer interactions are provided digitally.
