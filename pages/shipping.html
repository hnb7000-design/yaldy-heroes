Shipping & Delivery Information

How Shipping Works

We want your experience with YALDY™ to be clear, transparent, and worry-free. Below you'll find all the information regarding shipping, delivery timelines, and pre-order fulfillment.

Shipping Areas

We currently ship within Israel and to selected international destinations.

Israel Shipping

Shipping costs for orders within Israel are displayed at checkout. Deliveries to remote areas may require additional time or fees. Please contact us for details before placing an order.

International Shipping

International orders are shipped at a flat rate, clearly displayed at checkout. Shipping rates apply to standard delivery zones only.

Pre-Orders

Some of our products are offered as pre-orders. Estimated shipping timelines are displayed on each product page. By placing a pre-order, you reserve a product from our upcoming production run. You will receive email updates as your order progresses toward shipment.

Delivery Times

Delivery times are estimated from the moment the order is dispatched.

  • Israel: 5–10 business days
  • International: 10–21 business days

Delivery times may vary due to customs, holidays, or factors beyond our control.
