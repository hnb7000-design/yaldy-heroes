What is YALDY?

YALDY (Your Adventure Lies in Daring You) is a brand by Bible Heroes – Power of Truth. We create collectible puzzles featuring legendary biblical heroes, each with unique artwork, character lore cards, and hidden QR codes that unlock bonus digital content. It's an immersive experience that combines education, art, and discovery for the whole family.

What ages are the puzzles for?

Our puzzles are designed for ages 6 and up. Younger children can enjoy assembling the puzzles with parental help, while older kids and adults will appreciate the detailed artwork, character lore, and the QR scavenger hunt challenge. They make a great family activity!

What's included in each puzzle?

Each YALDY puzzle set includes: a premium-quality puzzle with original hero artwork, a collectible character lore card with the hero's story and abilities, a hidden QR code within the puzzle that unlocks exclusive digital bonus content, and a collector's guide. Everything comes in a beautifully designed keepsake box.

How does the QR bonus work?

Hidden within each completed puzzle is a QR code. Once you've assembled the puzzle, find and scan the QR code with your phone camera. This unlocks exclusive digital content: animated hero introductions, behind-the-scenes artwork, bonus lore about the hero's world, and sometimes special discount codes for future releases!

Do you ship internationally?

Currently, we offer domestic shipping within Israel with delivery in 5–7 business days. International shipping is available upon request. Please contact us at info@yaldyheroes.com for international shipping rates and estimated delivery times to your location.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 14-day return policy from the date of delivery. Items must be in their original, unopened packaging to be eligible for a return. To initiate a return, contact our team at info@yaldyheroes.com with your order number. Once your return is approved, we'll provide shipping instructions. Refunds are processed within 7–10 business days after we receive the item.

What are "Artifacts"?

Artifacts are the legendary items wielded by each YALDY hero. Each artifact has its own lore, unique abilities, and visual design. In the puzzle sets, artifacts are featured as special collectible elements. Some artifacts include David's Sling, Moses' Staff, Solomon's Ring, and more. As the YALDY Universe expands, new artifacts will be introduced with new heroes.

Will there be more heroes?

Absolutely! The YALDY Universe is ever-expanding. We have an exciting roadmap of new biblical heroes, each with their own puzzle sets, artifact lore, and digital bonus content. Follow us on social media and sign up for our newsletter to be the first to know about new hero releases and limited-edition collections.

Can I use YALDY puzzles for educational purposes?

Yes! YALDY puzzles are perfect for educational settings. Many teachers, homeschool families, and religious educators use them to bring biblical stories to life. The character lore cards provide age-appropriate historical and narrative context. For bulk educational orders, please contact us at info@yaldyheroes.com for special pricing.

How can I contact YALDY support?

You can reach our team by email at info@yaldyheroes.com. We aim to respond within 1–2 business days. For the latest news, sneak peeks, and community discussions, follow us on our social media channels. We love hearing from our community of heroes!
