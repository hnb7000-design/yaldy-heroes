Origin Story

THE QUESTION THAT
STARTED IT ALL

How one evening, one child, and one impossible question gave birth to a cinematic hero universe.

The Moment

IT STARTED WITH A QUESTION

It started with a question. My son looked up at me one evening and asked: 'Abba — who is stronger, Superman or David?'

I didn't have a quick answer. Superman is fictional. David was real. But no product on the shelf brought David to life the way Marvel and DC bring their heroes.

That question wouldn't leave me. What if the greatest heroes — the ones who shaped real history — could be experienced with the same power, the same cinematic intensity, the same awe?

The Birth of YALDY

BORN FROM THAT MOMENT

YALDY™ was born from that moment. A cinematic hero universe built around real heroes. Not to preach — but to inspire.

Every puzzle, every story, every visual — designed to make kids say: 'David is MY hero.'

“Real heroes don't wear capes. They carry slings.”
